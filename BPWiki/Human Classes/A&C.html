Administration and Command

In the world of Breach Protocol, the Administration and Command (A&C) class plays a crucial role in managing resources, coordinating teams, and ensuring the smooth operation of missions. This class is designed for players who enjoy strategic planning and leadership roles.

As an A&C player, you will have access to unique abilities that allow you to support your team, gather intelligence, and make critical decisions that can turn the tide of battle. Your skills in negotiation and resource management will be essential for success.

Make sure to utilize your abilities effectively and communicate with your team to achieve your objectives. The A&C class is not just about commanding others; it's also about being a team player and ensuring that everyone is working towards a common goal.
